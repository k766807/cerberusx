Teaching fit

AudienceUndergrad, graduate, capstone, AI safety, robotics, aerospace.
Time30–75 minutes for a class block; longer for seeded replay and JSON exports.
OutputScenario comparison, threshold defense, short writeup, or lab discussion.
Learning objective

Capability is not reliability.

Students compare a flexible mission AI against skeptical supervision and deterministic safety boundaries.

Exercise style

Break it on purpose.

Inject failures, compare runs, defend authority transfers, and explain why each layer should fail differently.

Magic number

Three is a magic number.

One AI flies, one AI argues, and one beautifully stubborn failsafe says absolutely not when physics gets rude.
